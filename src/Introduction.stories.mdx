# Araceae-ui

A simple adaptative component libary based on [.pulse Design System](https://pulse.heartbeat.ua/).
Focused on minimalism and versatility, making possible create unique interfaces focused on visual essentialism:
Just like the plants from [Araceae family](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Araceae),
calm and elegant. (they're awesome btw).
